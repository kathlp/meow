World Spay Day

Our Kapon Team was able to finish all the surgeries last night by 8 in the evening, bringing us to a total of 433 animals fixed for FREE. It may have been a very long wait for our recipients, but it was surely worth it, as their pets will not be multiplying from now on and will live happier and healthier lives. We are also very proud of our team PAWSxVIP for working the long, grueling hours to ensure that all animals will be accommodated for kapon. We are an organization not funded by the government, and we are only able to conduct these projects with the help of your donations.
