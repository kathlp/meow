Famous Foods & Drinks

Adobo

Adobo, a celebrated Filipino dish, combines vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices, offering a delightful taste experience cherished by many around the world.

Sago't Gulaman

Sago't gulaman, a cherished Filipino drink, melds tapioca pearls (sago) and agar jelly (gulaman) in a sweet caramelized syrup, delighting taste buds and quenching thirsts across the nation.
Learn More...

Lechon

Lechon, a Filipino favorite, is a whole roasted pig with crispy skin and tender meat, often the star of festive gatherings.
Learn More...

Halo-Halo

Halo-halo, a Filipino delight, blends shaved ice with sweet beans, fruits, and milk, offering a refreshing treat enjoyed by many during warm days.
